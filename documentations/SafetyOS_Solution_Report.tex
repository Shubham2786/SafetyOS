\section{Executive Summary}
SafetyOS is an AI-native intelligence layer for industrial operations. It fuses computer vision, OT/SCADA telemetry, IoT wearables, and enterprise workflows into a single explainable knowledge graph — turning latent signals into decisive foresight before an incident occurs.

\subsection{Thesis}
Industrial fatalities are not a data problem. They are a \textbf{fusion, reasoning and cognitive-load} problem. In every landmark incident of the last decade — from the Visakhapatnam Steel Plant coke-oven explosion of January 2025 to the refinery fires that preceded it — the signals existed. The intelligence layer did not.

SafetyOS closes that gap. It sits \textit{above} existing SCADA, EHS and CCTV infrastructure as a decision-support surface, never as an actuator. Humans and Safety-Instrumented Systems remain in the loop. The platform's job is foresight, explanation and orchestration.

\section{Problem Statement}
A modern refinery generates over one petabyte of operational data per year across CCTV, OT sensors, permits and mobile forms. Less than one percent is ever examined before an incident. The remaining 99\% becomes evidence — after the fact.

\begin{tcolorbox}[colback=LightMuted,colframe=NavyPrimary,arc=1mm,boxrule=0.5pt]
``The signals existed. The intelligence layer did not.''\\
\small\textit{— Root-cause finding, Visakhapatnam Steel Plant coke-oven explosion, January 2025}
\end{tcolorbox}

\subsection{Five Core Failure Modes}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Fusion Gap:} OT tags, camera events, permit state and worker location live in different systems. No engine reasons across them in real time.
    \item \textbf{Cognitive Overload:} A permit issuer today balances 12–20 checks per hot-work permit. Errors correlate with fatigue, not competence.
    \item \textbf{Alarm Fatigue:} Control rooms average 100+ alarms per operator per hour — an order of magnitude above the ISA-18.2 limit.
    \item \textbf{Handover Blind Spots:} Verbal handovers lose $\approx$40\% of hazard context without auditable transcripts.
    \item \textbf{Post-Incident Evidence Chaos:} Assembling evidence bundles takes days because logs live in ten separate databases.
\end{enumerate}

    \item \textbf{Apache Kafka:} High-throughput event broker processing 100,000+ sensor messages/second.
\end{itemize}

MATCH (a)-[:REQUIRES]->(p:PermitToWork {type: 'HOT_WORK'})
MATCH (p)-[:ENFORCES]->(l:LOTOProcedure)
WHERE l.status != 'VERIFIED_ZERO_ENERGY'
RETURN z.id AS zone_id, a.id AS asset_id, p.id AS permit_id, l.isolation_point AS isolation_point
ORDER BY p.risk_score DESC
